Naples Pool Renovation Services

Is your pool looking tired, dated, or in need of repair? Naples Pool Construction Company specializes in complete pool renovations that breathe new life into aging pools. From simple resurfacing to dramatic remodels, we have the expertise to transform your pool.

Many Naples homeowners inherit older pools when they buy a home, or their once-beautiful pool has simply aged over the years. Instead of living with an eyesore or spending a fortune on a new pool, renovation is often the smart choice.

Our Pool Renovation Services

  • Pool Resurfacing: Replace worn plaster with premium pebble, quartz, or aggregate finishes that last 15-20 years
  • Tile Replacement: Update waterline tile with modern glass, porcelain, or natural stone options
  • Coping Replacement: New coping in travertine, pavers, or natural stone for a fresh look
  • Equipment Upgrades: Modern pumps, filters, heaters, and salt systems for efficiency and convenience
  • LED Lighting: Color-changing LED lights that transform your pool at night
  • Water Feature Additions: Add waterfalls, fountains, bubblers, or deck jets
  • Spa Additions: Add a spa to your existing pool
  • Shape Modifications: Expand, reshape, or add features like sun shelves and tanning ledges
  • Automation: Smart pool controls for remote management via smartphone

Signs Your Pool Needs Renovation

  • Rough or stained pool surface
  • Cracked, chipped, or missing tiles
  • Outdated appearance or style
  • Frequent equipment breakdowns
  • High energy costs from old equipment
  • Leaks or structural concerns
  • Difficulty maintaining water chemistry

Pool Renovation Cost in Naples

Pool renovation costs vary widely based on the scope of work:

  • Resurfacing only: $8,000 - $15,000
  • Resurfacing + tile: $12,000 - $25,000
  • Complete renovation: $25,000 - $75,000+

We provide detailed estimates after assessing your pool's condition and discussing your goals. Every project is unique, and we'll help you prioritize improvements that fit your budget.

The Renovation Process

  1. Assessment: We inspect your pool and discuss your vision and budget
  2. Design & Planning: We create a renovation plan with material selections
  3. Drain & Prep: Pool is drained and surfaces are prepared
  4. Structural Repairs: Any cracks or damage are repaired
  5. Installation: New tile, coping, and finish are installed
  6. Equipment: Upgraded equipment is installed and tested
  7. Fill & Balance: Pool is filled and chemistry is balanced
  8. Walkthrough: Final inspection and pool school
