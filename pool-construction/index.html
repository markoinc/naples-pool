Naples Custom Pool Builders

At Naples Pool Construction Company, we specialize in building beautiful custom pools that transform your backyard into a private paradise. With over 20 years of experience and 500+ pools built throughout Collier County, we have the expertise to bring your vision to life.

Every pool we build is unique, designed specifically for your property, lifestyle, and budget. Whether you dream of a classic rectangular lap pool, a freeform tropical oasis, or a resort-style pool with all the bells and whistles, our team delivers exceptional results.

Our Pool Construction Process

  • Consultation & Design: We meet at your property to discuss your vision, assess the site, and create a custom design with 3D renderings
  • Permitting: We handle all Collier County permits and approvals
  • Excavation: Professional excavation with careful attention to your landscaping
  • Steel & Plumbing: Rebar framework and premium plumbing installation
  • Gunite/Shotcrete: High-strength shell construction built to last decades
  • Tile & Coping: Beautiful waterline tile and durable coping installation
  • Interior Finish: Premium pebble, quartz, or plaster finishes
  • Decking: Coordinated deck installation in pavers, travertine, or concrete
  • Equipment & Startup: Professional equipment installation and pool school

Pool Types We Build

  • In-ground gunite/concrete pools

  • Freeform natural pools
  • Geometric modern pools
  • Resort-style pools with beaches and sun shelves
  • Infinity/negative edge pools

Pool Construction Cost in Naples

Pool construction costs in Naples typically range from $50,000 to $150,000+ depending on size, design complexity, and features. During your free consultation, we'll provide a detailed estimate tailored to your specific project.

Factors that affect cost include:

  • Pool size and shape
  • Interior finish selection
  • Deck materials and size
  • Water features (waterfalls, fountains, etc.)
  • Spa additions
  • Lighting and automation
  • Site conditions and access
