Our Comprehensive Pool Construction Process

We've refined our construction process over 20+ years to ensure efficiency, quality, and complete client satisfaction. Here's what you can expect when you build with Naples Pool Construction Company:

  • Initial Consultation & Site Assessment We begin with a complimentary visit to your property where we discuss your vision, assess site conditions, evaluate access, and review any HOA or permitting considerations. This allows us to provide an accurate proposal tailored to your specific situation.
  • Custom Design & 3D Visualization Our design team creates detailed plans and photorealistic 3D renderings so you can visualize your pool before construction begins. We refine the design until it perfectly matches your expectations, including placement, shape, finishes, and features.
  • Permitting & HOA Approval We handle all Collier County building permits, electrical permits, and HOA submissions on your behalf. Our familiarity with local requirements ensures smooth approvals and avoids costly delays.
  • Excavation & Layout Professional excavation crews prepare your site with precision, protecting existing landscaping where possible and ensuring proper drainage. We stake out the exact pool location so you can approve placement before digging begins.
  • Steel & Plumbing Installation Heavy-gauge rebar is tied to exacting specifications, creating the structural framework that will support your pool for generations. Premium plumbing is installed with proper slope and connections for optimal circulation.
  • Gunite/Shotcrete Shell High-strength concrete is pneumatically applied to create a monolithic shell engineered to withstand Florida's soil conditions and water table. Our crews have applied thousands of shells throughout Collier County.
  • Tile, Coping & Finish Work Waterline tile, coping, and your chosen interior finish are installed by skilled artisans. We offer premium pebble, quartz, and plaster finishes in an array of colors to complement your design vision.
  • Decking & Surrounds Coordinated deck installation in premium pavers, natural travertine, or stamped concrete creates a seamless transition between pool and patio. Our decking stays cool underfoot—essential in Naples' climate.
  • Equipment Installation & Startup Energy-efficient pumps, filters, heaters, salt systems, and automation are professionally installed and configured. We fill your pool, balance water chemistry, and conduct thorough testing before handover.
  • Pool School & Final Walkthrough We provide comprehensive training on pool operation, maintenance, and equipment. You'll receive all documentation, warranties, and direct access to our team for any questions that arise.

Pool Types We Build

Our custom pool construction capabilities encompass virtually any design style or configuration. Here are some of the pool types Naples homeowners most frequently request:

Geometric & Modern Pools

Clean lines, precise angles, and contemporary aesthetics define our geometric pool designs. Rectangle, square, and L-shaped configurations pair beautifully with modern architecture and create excellent lap lanes for fitness swimming. These pools often feature vanishing edges, raised walls, and minimalist finishes that emphasize sophisticated simplicity.

Freeform & Natural Pools

Curved, organic shapes that mimic natural lagoons and ponds create a tropical paradise feel perfect for Naples' resort lifestyle. Freeform pools integrate seamlessly with lush landscaping, rock formations, and water features to create environments that feel like private sanctuaries hidden from the outside world.

Infinity & Negative Edge Pools

For properties with views—whether overlooking the Gulf, a golf course, or a preserve—infinity edge pools create the stunning visual effect of water merging with the horizon. These sophisticated designs require precise engineering and expert construction, areas where our experience truly shines.

Resort-Style Pools

Sun shelves (Baja shelves), beach entries, swim-up bars, and integrated spas characterize our resort-style designs. These pools are designed for entertaining and family enjoyment, with multiple zones for different activities and age groups.

Lap Pools & Exercise Pools

Serious swimmers appreciate our lap pool designs, which maximize swimming lanes within available space. We also install swim-in-place systems and resistance jets for properties where a full-length lap pool isn't feasible.

Cocktail Pools (Spools)

For smaller properties or as secondary pools, cocktail pools—also known as spools—provide full-size pool features in a compact footprint. These intimate designs often include spa features and are perfect for relaxation and entertaining.

Pool Construction Costs in Naples, FL

Investment ranges for custom pool construction in Naples vary based on size, design complexity, features, and finishes. Here are general guidelines to help you plan:

Typical Investment Ranges

Basic In-Ground Pool (400-500 sq ft) $65,000 – $85,000
Mid-Range Custom Pool (500-700 sq ft) $85,000 – $125,000
Luxury Custom Pool (700+ sq ft) $125,000 – $200,000
Resort-Style with Premium Features $200,000+

Factors influencing cost include pool size and shape, interior finish selection (plaster, pebble, or quartz), deck material and square footage, water features, spa additions, equipment tier, automation systems, and site conditions. During your complimentary consultation, we'll provide a detailed estimate tailored to your specific project.
