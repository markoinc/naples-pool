About Naples Pool Construction

Building dream pools in Southwest Florida for over 20 years

Naples' Trusted Pool Builders

Naples Pool Construction Company was founded with a simple mission: to build beautiful, lasting pools that bring joy to Southwest Florida families. Over the past two decades, we've had the privilege of creating over 500 custom pools throughout Collier County.

What sets us apart? We're not just contractors – we're craftsmen who take pride in every project. From the initial design consultation to the final inspection, we treat your property like it's our own.

Our team includes Florida-licensed pool contractors, experienced designers, and skilled craftsmen who bring years of expertise to every project. We stay current with the latest pool technology and trends to offer you the best options for your backyard oasis.

We believe in transparent communication, fair pricing, and standing behind our work. That's why we offer comprehensive warranties and maintain relationships with our clients long after the pool is finished.
