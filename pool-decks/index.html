Pool Deck Material Options

Travertine Pavers

Natural travertine is the gold standard for luxury pool decks in Naples, and for good reason. This classic Mediterranean stone offers unmatched elegance, naturally cool surface temperatures, and the timeless beauty that improves with age.

  • Heat Resistance: Travertine's porous structure and light colors reflect sunlight rather than absorbing it, staying significantly cooler than darker materials.
  • Slip Resistance: The natural texture provides excellent traction when wet—essential for pool surrounds.
  • Durability: Properly sealed travertine withstands decades of Florida weather, pool chemicals, and heavy use.
  • Aesthetic Versatility: Available in ivory, walnut, silver, gold, and multi-color blends to complement any architectural style.
  • Premium Appeal: Travertine signals quality and sophistication, enhancing property value.

We source premium-grade travertine from trusted quarries and install it with precision, ensuring proper bedding, joint spacing, and sealing for lasting performance.

Brick Pavers

Interlocking concrete and clay brick pavers offer exceptional durability, design flexibility, and value. Modern paver technology has produced materials that combine the traditional look of brick with enhanced performance characteristics perfect for pool applications.

  • Design Options: Dozens of shapes, sizes, colors, and patterns enable truly custom designs. Herringbone, basketweave, running bond, and circular patterns create distinctive looks.
  • Repairability: Individual pavers can be replaced if damaged without disturbing surrounding areas—a significant advantage over poured surfaces.
  • Permeability: Properly installed paver systems allow water to drain between joints, reducing runoff and preventing puddling.
  • Cool Paver Options: Specialized cool pavers with reflective pigments stay up to 20 degrees cooler than standard pavers.
  • Value: Pavers deliver excellent aesthetics and performance at a range of price points.

We install pavers from leading manufacturers including Belgard, Tremron, Artistic Pavers, and others, and can match or complement existing hardscaping.

Stamped Concrete

Stamped or imprinted concrete delivers the look of natural stone, brick, or tile at a more accessible price point. Modern stamping and coloring techniques create remarkably realistic textures that fool even discerning eyes.

  • Seamless Installation: Large monolithic pours eliminate joints where weeds can grow or settlement can occur.
  • Pattern Variety: Ashlar slate, flagstone, cobblestone, brick, and wood plank patterns offer diverse aesthetic options.
  • Color Integration: Integral colors and surface-applied stains create depth and natural variation.
  • Cost Efficiency: Stamped concrete typically costs less than natural stone while achieving similar visual impact.
  • Customization: Borders, medallions, and pattern combinations create unique designs.

Our concrete craftsmen have decades of combined experience and bring genuine artistry to every pour.

Cool Deck & Spray Deck

Acrylic spray-deck systems applied over concrete create comfortable, slip-resistant surfaces with excellent heat reflectivity. These coatings are particularly popular for pool decks because they address the primary concern of hot surfaces.

  • Surface Temperature: Cool deck coatings can be 30% cooler than plain concrete—a meaningful difference in Naples' summer heat.
  • Texture Options: From subtle knockdown to aggressive orange peel, texture provides slip resistance and visual interest.
  • Color Palette: Wide range of colors from classic white to earth tones.
  • Resurfacing Option: Can be applied over existing concrete decks to refresh appearance and improve performance.
  • Maintenance: Recoating every 5-10 years maintains appearance and protection.

  • Bluestone: Blue-gray tones that complement contemporary architecture

Pool Deck Design Considerations

Creating the ideal pool deck involves more than selecting materials. Our design process addresses multiple factors:

Size & Proportions

Deck sizing depends on how you'll use the space. Generous decking allows for lounge chairs, dining areas, and circulation without crowding. We typically recommend a minimum of 6-8 feet of deck width around pools, with larger gathering areas where entertaining will occur.

Drainage & Grade

Proper slope directs water away from your home and pool while preventing standing water that creates slip hazards and promotes algae growth. Our installations incorporate appropriate grades and, where necessary, trench drains or channel systems.

Integration with Landscape

Pool decks should flow naturally into surrounding landscape elements. We coordinate with landscapers and work around existing features to create cohesive outdoor environments.

Expansion Joints & Movement

Florida's temperature swings cause materials to expand and contract. Proper joint placement and flexible sealants accommodate this movement without cracking or damage.

Safety Features

Non-slip textures, proper lighting provisions, and code-compliant step designs ensure your deck is safe for all users, including children and elderly family members.
