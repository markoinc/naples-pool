Naples Pool Deck Contractors

Your pool deck is more than just a surface – it's the foundation of your outdoor living space. At Naples Pool Construction Company, we design and install stunning pool decks that complement your pool, withstand Florida's climate, and stay cool underfoot.

Whether you're building a new pool or upgrading an existing deck, we offer a variety of premium materials and designs to create the perfect outdoor environment for your family.

Pool Deck Materials

  • Pavers: Versatile, durable, and available in countless styles and colors. Easy to repair and replace. Popular options include brick, concrete, and porcelain pavers.
  • Travertine: Natural stone with a luxurious, elegant appearance. Stays cool in the sun and is slip-resistant. Perfect for upscale pool areas.
  • Stamped Concrete: Cost-effective option that mimics the look of stone, brick, or tile. Sealed for durability and available in many patterns.
  • Cool Deck/Kool Deck: Spray-applied textured coating that stays significantly cooler than standard concrete. Great for families with children.
  • Natural Stone: Options like flagstone, bluestone, and limestone for a one-of-a-kind look.
  • Concrete Overlay: Resurface existing concrete with decorative coatings and textures.

Why Pool Deck Material Matters in Florida

In Naples' hot climate, deck surface temperature is crucial. We specialize in materials and techniques that:

  • Stay cooler under direct sunlight
  • Provide slip resistance when wet
  • Withstand salt, chlorine, and chemicals
  • Resist cracking and settling
  • Complement your home's architecture
  • Add value to your property

Pool Deck Cost in Naples

Pool deck costs depend on material choice, deck size, and site conditions:

  • Concrete/Cool Deck: $8 - $15 per sq ft installed
  • Stamped Concrete: $12 - $20 per sq ft installed
  • Pavers: $15 - $30 per sq ft installed
  • Travertine: $20 - $40 per sq ft installed
