Premium Pool Features for Naples Homes

Transform your pool from ordinary to extraordinary with luxury features that create a resort-like experience in your own backyard. Naples Pool Construction Company specializes in designing and installing premium pool additions that enhance both beauty and functionality.

Whether you're building a new pool or upgrading an existing one, these features add value, visual interest, and enjoyment to your outdoor living space.

Water Features

  • Rock Waterfalls: Natural-looking rock formations with cascading water create a tropical paradise feel. Custom designed to fit your pool's style.
  • Sheer Descent Waterfalls: Elegant sheets of water falling from raised walls or features. Modern and sophisticated.
  • Grottos: Walk-behind waterfalls with seating areas for a truly resort-style experience.
  • Fountains: Deck jets, laminar jets, and bubbler fountains add movement and sound.
  • Scuppers & Spillways: Water features that flow from raised walls or spa overflows.
  • Rain Curtains: Dramatic water walls that create a stunning visual barrier.

Fire Features

  • Fire Bowls: Stunning fire bowls on pedestals that reflect beautifully on the water.
  • Fire Pits: Integrated fire pits near the pool for evening ambiance.
  • Fire & Water Bowls: Combination features with fire above and water cascading below.
  • Linear Fire Features: Modern linear burners along pool edges or walls.

Lighting

  • LED Pool Lights: Color-changing lights that transform your pool at night with unlimited color options.
  • Fiber Optic Lighting: Create starfield effects on pool floors and walls.
  • Landscape Lighting: Coordinated outdoor lighting that highlights your pool area.
  • Underwater Accent Lights: Highlight features, steps, and benches.

Spas & Relaxation

  • Attached Spas: Raised spas that spill over into the main pool.
  • Standalone Spas: Separate hot tubs with independent temperature control.
  • Sun Shelves/Tanning Ledges: Shallow areas for lounging and relaxation.
  • Baja Shelves: In-pool seating areas perfect for chairs and umbrellas.
  • Swim-Up Bars: Integrated bars with underwater seating.

Other Premium Features

  • Beach Entries: Zero-depth entry that mimics a natural beach.
  • Infinity/Negative Edges: Vanishing edges that create a stunning visual effect.
  • Underwater Sound Systems: Enjoy music while swimming.
  • Pool Automation: Control everything from your smartphone.
  • Slides & Diving Boards: Fun features for families.
